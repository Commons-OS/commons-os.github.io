How It Will Work

A glimpse into the Context Engine workflow.

1

Describe Your Context

Answer guided questions about your organization, city, or project. Upload relevant documents. The more context you provide, the more tailored the recommendations become.

2

AI Builds Your Blueprint

The Context Engine analyzes your inputs, identifies key dimensions (size, culture, industry, maturity, constraints), and creates a contextual blueprint ‚Äî a structured representation of your unique situation.

3

Patterns Get Adapted

Patterns from the Pattern Engine are filtered, ranked, and adapted based on your blueprint. You see which patterns are most relevant, how they've worked in similar contexts (via Lighthouse Engine), and how to adapt them to your reality.

4

Evolve Over Time

Your context isn't static. The Context Engine tracks changes, learns from your implementation experiences, and continuously refines its recommendations as your situation evolves.
